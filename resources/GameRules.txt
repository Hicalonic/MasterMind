
Your goal, as Decoder, is to crack the secret Code provided by CodeMaster, with the least number of attempts.
You have a maximum of 12 attempts.

The Code can be made up of any combination of five colors.
The MasterCode can use two or more equal colors, in different positions.

You have to duplicate the exact colors and positions of the hidden Code.

Type /help to see all the available commands.

Follow the color guide to enter your attempts and receive the feedback from the CodeMaster after every guess.

The server ends when the Decoder guesses the secret Code.
